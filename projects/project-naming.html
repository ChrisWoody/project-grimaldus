Project Naming

I've liked Warhammer 40K for a while, and it was the video games that got me into it (Dawn of War series, Fire Warrior, Space Marine to name a few). Around the time I was starting to read books, I learnt that there was also Warhammer books (only knew of the video games and models). A quick Google and I came across Black Library and I was blown away by this whole other side of Warhammer I wasn't aware of, and with thousands of books to read, where do I even start? I came across a series of books called Space Marine Battles which were set in 40K times that spanned a mixture of locations and legions so I went with that.

So as a bit of a nod to the books that got me more into reading, I thought I could incorporate them into the programming projects I take on by naming them from the the main or most important character in the book.
